Balloon Pop Numbers

Pop the balloons in the correct numerical order! Practice your number sequencing skills.

Objective

Pop balloons from smallest to largest number

Reward

Earn 30 XP for completing the game

Level 1
Score 0
Balloons Left 10

Click the balloons in order from smallest to largest number!

How to Play

  1. Click the Start Game button to begin
  2. Balloons with numbers will appear on screen
  3. Pop the balloons in order from smallest to largest number
  4. You earn points for each correct balloon popped
  5. If you pop a balloon out of order, you'll lose a life
  6. Complete all levels to earn maximum XP!
Tip: Read numbers carefully. Larger numbers have more digits!
