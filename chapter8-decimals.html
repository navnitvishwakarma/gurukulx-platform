Understanding Decimals

Decimals are another way of expressing fractions with denominators 10, 100, 1000, etc.

Tenths and Hundredths

  • Tenths: 1/10 is written as 0.1.
  • Hundredths: 1/100 is written as 0.01.

Place value chart extends to the right of the ones place: Tenths, Hundredths, Thousandths.

Comparing Decimals

To compare decimals, we compare digits from left to right. If whole number parts are equal, compare tenths, then hundredths, and so on.

Example: 0.5 > 0.05

Money and Decimals

We use decimals to represent money. 100 paise = 1 Rupee.

  • 1 paise = 1/100 Rupee = ₹ 0.01
  • 50 paise = ₹ 0.50

Applications

Decimals are used in measuring length (cm to m), weight (g to kg), and money.
