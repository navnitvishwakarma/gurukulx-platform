Arithmetic Mean, Range, Mode, and Median

0% Complete

Data Analysis

Arithmetic Mean

Mean is the sum of all observations divided by the number of observations.

Mean = (Sum of observations) / (Number of observations)

Range

Range is the difference between the highest and lowest observation.

Mode and Median

Mode: The observation that occurs most often.

Median: The middle value when data is arranged in ascending or descending order.

Solved Examples

Example 1: Finding Mean

Question: Find mean of 1, 2, 3, 4, 5

Solution: Sum = 15. Count = 5. Mean = 15/5 = 3.

Example 2: Finding Mode

Question: Data: 1, 1, 2, 3, 4, 1, 2. Find Mode.

Solution: 1 occurs most frequently (3 times). Mode is 1.
