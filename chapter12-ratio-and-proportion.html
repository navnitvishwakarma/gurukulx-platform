Ratio and Proportion

We often need to compare quantities in our daily life. Ratio is a way to compare by division.

Ratio

The ratio of two quantities of the same kind and in the same unit is the fraction that one quantity is of the other.

The ratio 3 to 2 is written as 3:2.

Proportion

If two ratios are equal, we say they are in proportion.

Represented as a:b :: c:d or a:b = c:d.

Here, a and d are extreme terms, b and c are middle terms.

Unitary Method

The method in which we first find the value of one unit and then the value of required number of units is known as the unitary method.

Solved Examples

Example 1: Finding Ratio

Question: Find ratio of 50cm to 2m.

Solution:

Convert both to same unit. 2m = 200cm.

Ratio = 50 : 200

Simplifying (divide by 50): 1 : 4

Example 2: Unitary Method

Question: Cost of 6 pens is ₹30. What is cost of 10 pens?

Solution:

Cost of 6 pens = ₹30

Cost of 1 pen = 30 ÷ 6 = ₹5

Cost of 10 pens = 5 × 10 = ₹50
